About Me

I'm Nina Jabari
Creative designer

Nina is my name. I am a graphic designer who have been around advertising agencies for more than 13 years. I've been doing artworks since my childhood. During the past years I was a Graphic Designer in an airline working with a team of talented people and also doing all kind of production, multimedia, marketing, and print design.
I have especial experiences to design challenging and wide range of projects during 13 years, Design Guideline, Seatback Screens GUI & AFI on Plane, Design Commemorative stamp, Aircraft livery design, Airplane’s emergency card, …
Always I try to learn new things, today one of my missions is improving my skill in UI and UX because I passioned about it!
I have never got scared about saying my ideas, whenever I start a new project, the project becomes to like as my child and I enjoy during it grows up. This is me!
