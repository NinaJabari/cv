Designing ID card & holder ribbon

  • Client : IranAir Airline
  • Date : 2010
  • Skills : Illustrator, CorelDRAW, Photoshop

The important thing for airlines is security and for all of the staff, pilots and flight attendant to be known by ID card, ID card design of stewardesses and pilots with ideas in materials and type of design for security and to prevent id cards counterfeiting was a big challenge. After knowing materials and making an idea the design was started, Id card was designed by a pieas of transparent plase around the symbol of logo furthermore hologram design and real flight routemap can be seen by ultraviolet light on the card.

And after that holder ribbon was designe with harmony by ID card design.
