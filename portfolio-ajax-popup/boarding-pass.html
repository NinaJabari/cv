Boarding pass design

  • Client : IranAir Airline
  • Date : 2015
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop, CorelDRAW

Boarding pass was another project full of challenges, before redesign there was not any design on boarding-pass, due to the organization in the printing of variable characteristics and also the kind of printer due to the special grid for the location of the text and image, we had many limitations in the design. In this project, we tried to improve these problems and irregularity of the text in flight cards while keeping its visual wellness. The design was finally designed and implemented using a dedicated motif (a traditional motif from the analyzed design of Sepahsalar Mosque, it was reached out to a unique motif) this motif was used to make uniform in-flight designs.
