Special design for Carpet in front of the ticket counter to welcome "Homa" airline special passengers

  • Client : IranAir Sales Office In Charles de Gaulle Airport
  • Date : 2015
  • Skills : Illustrator

Ordered by the head of the sales office of the National Homa Airline in Paris. It was one of the my interest projects, this design with a combination made by traditional Iranian motifs and the airline logo was placed at Charles de Gaulle Airport Paris in front of the ticket counter to welcome "Homa" airline special passengers.
