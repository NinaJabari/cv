Designing catering package in flight for all Seasons

  • Client : Airport services IranAir airline
  • Date : 2015-2016
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop, CorelDRAW

In during one-year project of designing the catering packages design, we decided to make a harmony in the color theme of the interior design airplane cabin, this harmony was between the lighting interior in airplane cabin, the multimedia “GUI” the aircraft Seat and flight attendent uniform and seat cover etc. the color theme was changed in evry season.
