Certificate degree design for Iran University of Science & Technology

  • Client : Iran University of Science & Technology
  • Date : 2016
  • Skills : Illustrator

The design was done exclusively and due to the high importance of the certificate (university degree), a complex design was designed along with a hologram on the text. The design of lines with different thicknesses and the use of colors palette on the lines also tried to prevent document forgery.
