Designing a steel personal badge pin - Airport Service staff

  • Client : Airport Service
  • Date : 2019
  • Skills : Illustrator

Enamel pin design for airport services inside the airport ramp and flight crew staff, all efforts were made special design because they have a direct connection with passengers.

First, we started to design curved and soft cutting molds on matte steel material, as well as the combination of enameling with metal, which is one of the characteristics of the Iranian and national art of Iran Air Airline "Homa". all of the this kind of design were for making good feeling to the passengers when they meet iran air staff.
