Iranair Guideline Design

  • Client : Iran International Airline / Iran Air "HOMA"
  • Date : 2016
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign

One of the problems in the design studio of “Homa” Airlines was the lack of guidelines for creating harmony in designs, there was only one color code for the logo. But brand guidelines go far beyond a logo or icon. It's in their colors, imagery, fonts, tone, and even the feeling you get when you see a design. Therefore, the suggestion of preparing a proposal and persuading the company's need for guideline design, was started with the creation of a group. Due to the huge company, there was a need to hold different meetings with different parts of the company, such as Engineering and Repairs, Airport Services and Ticket Offices, etc., to collect information.

And it was decided to create guidelines in three parts: web and multimedia, print and in-flight design. some of the pages of “print” guideline design can be seen below.:
