Typography logo design

  • Client : Saleh Travel Agency
  • Date : 2015
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop

The first logo for Saleh travel agency was designed in the traditional persian language, we used the name agency "saleh" in persian language as "صالح". The client liked to use the Persian word with the traditional iranian font "Nastaliq". The logo was designed by typography, a combination of font and form.
