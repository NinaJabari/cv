Map Design

  • Client : IranAir Airline
  • Date : 2019
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop, CorelDRAW

Designing advertising environments in-flight and entry terminals was presented in a proposal. One of the ideas was the map of the capital of Iran - Tehran, along with the introduction of tourist places, important tourist attractions, metro lines, emergency contacts, etc.
All designs, symbols, icons, etc. were specially designed during four months. This tourism package was provided to the passengers in a cover in the seat pockets of the plane, which included the airport guide map, the tourist guide map of Tehran and also the design of advertising environments.

Material: waterproof stonepaper
