Saleh Travel Agency / Rebranding

  • Client : Saleh Travel Agency
  • Date : 2017
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, 3D Model

Saleh travel agency rebranding project. includes logo design, Letterhead set - Stationery design, website design, Interior design consultation of the office, badge pin design for children and adults, luggage tag, etc.

At first we needed a symbol or design that represents the travel agency for developing the logo and to have an eye for design, we used the name agency ”saleh” in persian language it was " صالح ", the client liked to use the persian word. The logo was designed by typography, a combination of font and format, after that we reached the Color scheme, a combination of 2 colors consistently were used in the agency branding (in addition to black and white logos). Color scheme will be a part of their style guide, and ensures consistency as you design brand collateral across different platforms and in this time we designed ideas with better standpoint… (pictured and highlighted below)
