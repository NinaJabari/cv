commemorative stamp Design / for the 50th anniversary of Iran's national airline "Homa"

  • Client : Iran Air Airline
  • Date : August 10, 2011
  • Skills : Photoshop, Illustrator

Designing a commemorative stamp for the 50th anniversary of Iran's national airline "Homa" tasked by the Ministry of road and transportation. At first, the proposal was prepared. The plan includes: postcard envelope, "Homa" 50th anniversary postcard moreover commercial envelop size "DL" for the whole package was approved. In a preliminary study By adapting the cultural elements of Iran, the gilding, motif, calligraphy and... the sketchs were done. the value of this design is more for the hand drawing in parts of the gilding design.

Considering all these things in the design, I tried to pay attention to the details that are characteristic of Iranian art in the design (the logo of Iran Air "Homa" fifty years ago was inspired by the designs Achaemenid Empire)
