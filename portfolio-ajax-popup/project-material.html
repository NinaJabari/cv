Textile design flight attendant's scarf

  • Client : Iran Air airport services
  • Date : 2018-2019
  • Skills : Illustrator, Photoshop

In during one-year project of designing the scarf of the flight attendant uniform, we decided to make a harmony in the color theme of the interior design airplane cabin, this harmony was between catering package design, the lighting interior in airplane cabin, the multimedia “GUI” the aircraft Seat and etc. the color theme was changed in evry season.

In the designs scarf, motifs corresponding to the "Homa" logo were designed first, and the designs were accomplished in accordance with the color theme of each season changes during one year.
