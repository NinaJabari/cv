Facade design of airline ticket sales office building

  • Client : IranAir Airline
  • Date : 2016
  • Skills : Illustrator, CorelDRAW

The airline logo design was Inspired by an image atop one of the columns at Persepolis, a series of ancient Iranian motifs whose most significant inspiration was Homa, the Persian mythological griffin, which can be found in prosperous Persepolis. According to some accounts, the griffin never rests, living its entire life flying invisibly high above the earth.
That's why, in the Facade design of Airline ticket sales office building "Homa" National Airlines, special attention was paid to the history of the logo, and the design of the sales office columns, which was inspired by the ancient columns of Persepolis.
